In this post, we will walk through the steps of sewing a button for beginners. You'll learn how to select the right materials and the basic sewing technique for securing a button to your fabric.

Materials Needed

  • Button
  • Needle
  • Thread
  • Scissors
  • Fabric

Step 1: Choosing the Button

Select a button that complements your fabric. Ensure it’s the correct size for your buttonhole.


Step 2: Threading the Needle

Thread the needle with a piece of thread about 18 inches long. Tie a knot at the end of the thread.


Step 3: Sewing the Button

Place the button on the fabric where you want to attach it. Insert the needle through the fabric and one of the button's holes, then go through the opposite hole. Repeat until the button is secured.


Step 4: Finishing

Once the button is secure, tie off the thread on the back of the fabric and trim any excess.
