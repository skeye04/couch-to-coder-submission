Cozy Crochet Blanket


This easy crochet blanket pattern is perfect for beginners. With simple stitches and clear instructions, youâ€™ll be able to create a warm and cozy blanket.

Materials Needed:

  • Crochet hook (size 6mm)
  • Yarn (medium weight)
  • Scissors
  • Tapestry needle

Step 1: Chain Stitch

Start by making a chain of 100 stitches. This will determine the width of your blanket.


Step 2: Double Crochet

In the second chain from the hook, make a double crochet. Continue double crocheting in each chain stitch across the row.


Step 3: Repeat

For each row, chain 1 and turn your work. Continue making double crochets in each stitch until the blanket reaches your desired length.
